METHOD     (ACTIV) The target material consisted of GaAs foils    14866001   24 
           encapsulated in aluminum irradiation containers,       14866001   25 
           along with Au monitor foils, and positioned in the     14866001   26 
           NIF chamber during high neutron yield shots. Both NIF  14866001   27 
           shots utilized in this work were part of the HYBRID-E  14866001   28 
           campaign, which developed the first capsule design to  14866001   29 
           reach ignition and now regularly performs NIF shots    14866001   30 
           with total neutron yields >10**17. There were two      14866001   31 
           positions in the NIF chamber used for these            14866001   32

           times over two days. One detector was used for each    14866001   37 
           TOAD. Count times ranged from 60 minutes to 6.5 hours  14866001   38 
           with the goal of achieving ~1% counting statistics.    14866001   39 
ANALYSIS   Based on the nuclear counting data from the Au         14866001   40 
           isotopes and the calculated cross section for the      14866001   41 
           197Au(n, 2n) reaction, the spectrum averaged 75As(n,   14866001   42 
           2n) cross section can be calculated by a ratio method  14866001   43 
           as A74/A196 = (A75*Int Phi(E)*sigma75(E) dE)/          14866001   44 
            (A197*Int Phi(E)sigma197(E)dE),                       14866001   45 
           sigma75 = (A197*sigma197/A196)*(A74/A75), where Am is  14866001   46 
           the atom (A) of an isotope with mass number m, Phi is  14866001   47 
           the time-integrated neutron flux (neutron cm**-2),     14866001   48 
           and sigma is the cross section (cm2).                  14866001   49
