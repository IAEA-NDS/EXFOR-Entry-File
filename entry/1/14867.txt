           ray intensities were analyzed using the rate equation  14867001   62 
           for a simple capture and decay process, which gives    14867001   63 
           a(t) = R(1-exp(-lambda*ti))exp(-lambda*t), where a     14867001   64 
           is the activity at time t after the end of the         14867001   65 
           irradiation, R is the reaction rate, and ti is the     14867001   66 
           irradiation time. The reaction rate can be expressed   14867001   67 
           as R = N0*(sigma*phith+I*phiepi), where N0 is the      14867001   68 
           number of target atoms (assumed constant), sigma       14867001   69

           the effective resonance integral, and phith and        14867001   71
