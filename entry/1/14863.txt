ANALYSIS   The transmission cross sections were calculated using  14863001   55 
           ratios of counts from a full sample cell and an empty  14863001   56 
           sample cell, Cs and Ce, respectively, normalized by    14863001   57 
           similar counts from an upstream monitor counting (at   14863001   58 
           the same neutron energy) Ms and Me: sigma(E) =         14863001   59 
            (1/rhom)*ln((Ce(E)*Ms(E))/(Cs(E)*Me(E))), where rhom  14863001   60 
           is the areal density (in atoms or molecules/barn).     14863001   61 
           The resulting transmission must be scaled with an      14863001   62 
           areal number density to arrive at a microscopic cross  14863001   63 
           section. Should that areal density not be uniform      14863001   64 
           across the sample, corrections might need to be made,  14863001   65 
           especially when the transmission of the sample is low  14863001   66 
           or strongly varying in energy.                         14863001   67
